FeQtA

About

FeQtA translates to For every Question there's an Answer. At FeQtA we make sure that you get answer for your every question related to competetive examinations. FeQtA is built for aspiring Indian students who want to clear their examinations with flying colors as well as for the experienced professionals who can share their valuable knowledge.

FeQtA provides you with following features -

  • Follow topics of your choice.
  • Follow your favourite writers and make your feed better.
  • Follow questions and get their answers in your feed when other FeQtA users answer it.
  • Rank based system for knowing the top writers.
